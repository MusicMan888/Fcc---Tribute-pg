Tommy Bolin

So much more than a replacement guitarist

Here is a summary of Tommy's major milestones

  • August 1, 1951 - Born in Souix City, Iowa
  • 1968-1971 - Member of Zephyr, Colorado based blues rock group who released 2 albums "Zephyr" and "Going Back To Colorado"
  • 1972 - Created heavy metal jazz fusion group, Energy.
  • 1973 - Played on Billy Cobham's Spectrum Album.
  • 1973-1974 - Member of The James Gang, who released 2 albums "Bang" and "Miami"
  • 1974 - Played on Alphonse Mouzons' album "Mind Transplant"
  • 1975 - Member of Deep Purple, who released the album "Come Taste The Band"
  • 1975 - Released his first solo album "Teaser"
  • 1976 - Released his Second solo album "Private Eyes"
  • December 3, 1976 - Dies in Miami, Florida during the first stop on tour, opening for Jeff Beck.
